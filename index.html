ROSTER

    • Miami native Lucas Rego, better know as LooKas, has made impressive waves in the electronic dance music scene since late 2013. Ever since his groundbreaking remix of "Tsunami" by DVBBS and Borgeous was released, it has garnered over half a million steams in under three months. Moreover, the remix was commissioned by Ministry of Sound and released as an official mix in early 2014. Since then, he has debuted his original single "Anger" on BBC Radio 1's Diplo & Friends. Known for his signature festival sound, Billboard.com's "Next Big Sound" was quick to recognize LooKas as one of the top 10 artists on their Rising Artist Chart. Furthermore, house heavyweights such as Tiesto, Carnage, Dillon Francis and more have already actively supported his productions.

    • Jacob Grant appears as your average 16-year-old Aussie, studying in Year 11 and finding casual employment at a local toy store. Yet over the past seven months the young producer, charmingly dubbed Just A Gent, has kept his Soundcloud profile steadily populated with an impressive catalogue of original works and remixes.

    Charting at #1 on music blog aggregator Hype Machine, his remix of American Authors' "Best Day of My Life" garnered over half a million plays in 30 days prior to being picked up by Island Def Jam. His music has already received support by the largest names in electronic dance music including Skrillex, Diplo, Dillon Francis, Flume and more, whilst having received airplay on BBC's 1Xtra and Pete Tong's Evolution Radio.

    2014 is only destined to get bigger for Just A Gent with an official remix for Madison Avenue's #1 hit "Don't Call Me Baby" and an intense touring schedule.
